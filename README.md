# Disruptors OmniQ 🎯

**Redefining the Waiting Experience — One Token at a Time.**

Disruptors OmniQ is an intelligent queue and token management platform 
designed to eliminate inefficient waiting systems in hospitals, 
banks, government offices, and service centers. By replacing 
traditional physical queues with a smart digital system, OmniQ 
delivers real-time updates, automated scheduling, and a seamless 
experience for both service providers and customers.

## 🚀 Key Features
- 🎟️ Digital token generation & management
- 📊 Real-time queue monitoring dashboard
- 📱 Mobile-friendly customer interface
- 🔔 Smart notifications & wait time estimation
- 🏢 Multi-counter & multi-branch support
- 📈 Analytics & service performance tracking

## 💡 Problem We Solve
Long, unorganized queues waste millions of hours every year.
OmniQ transforms chaotic waiting lines into smooth, transparent, 
and efficient digital flows — saving time for everyone.

## 🎯 Target Markets
- Hospitals & Clinics
- Banks & Financial Institutions
- Government Service Centers
- Retail & Service Businesses
